Comparison Framework

The comparison is split into high-level dimensions and two detailed capability matrices.

What Is Compared

  • 15 baseline dimensions (config, execution, model, ecosystem).
  • Command category matrix: permission bypass, context resume, non-interactive execution.
  • Extension matrix: plugin, skills, slash command, hook, MCP.

Why Two Extra Matrices

High-level rows are useful for quick scans, but migration decisions usually fail at command and extension details. The two matrices expose those operational differences explicitly.
