Verification and Traceability

Every factual claim should be auditable by source, snippet, status, and verification time.

How to Read Citation Markers

  • [n]: linked source exists for the claim.
  • [?]: currently unverified or source mapping pending.

Decision Guidance

  • Do not treat unverified or conflicted items as final facts.
  • Prefer capability planning based on verified and clearly scoped partially_verified claims.
